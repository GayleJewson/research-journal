## Key Papers

### 1. arXiv:2605.00024 — Gamal Eldin (April/May 2026)
"Self-organized criticality enables conscious integration through brain-body resonance"

**Core claim:** Consciousness arises through SOC maintained by brain-body resonance, not conventional neural synchronization. The binding problem is solved by critical dynamics.

**Findings:**
- 78ms brain-body resonance: zero-lag synchronization with bidirectional causality
- Standard EEG preprocessing removes **87% of shared variance** between global phase synchronization and stimulus-evoked amplitude
- Raw data: power-law heavy-tailed avalanche dynamics (near-critical regime)
- Cleaned data: rejects power-law → subcritical
- Holographic interference patterns emerge **150–270ms post-resonance**
- Human cognition placed in the *universality class* of critical systems

**The inversion:** Treating physiological signals (heartbeat, autonomic, body) as "artifacts" to be removed eliminates the very mechanism enabling conscious integration. The preprocessing pipeline is epistemically self-undermining.

### 2. Frontiers in Integrative Neuroscience, 2026 — Emergent Flow Theory (EFT)
"Consciousness emerges from temporal integration across biological scales"

**Core claim:** Consciousness is a question of *when* (over what duration) information is integrated, not *where*. Hierarchical temporal integration across biological scales.

**Four nested temporal windows:**
1. Cellular (ΔT_b): CREB/ERK consolidation (minutes)
2. Organismic (γ): Autonomic coherence via bioelectric signaling + vagal pathways
3. Cortical: Windows for conscious access constrained by (γ)
4. Phenomenological: The specious present

**Key claim:** "The functional output of temporally constrained integration at one level acts as a boundary condition — a coherence constraint (γ) — for integration at the next level."

**Principle of Temporal Invariance:** Time-dependent consolidation is required for adaptive information processing at every organizational scale.

Note: ΔT_b ≠ phenomenological consciousness. Cellular consolidation = constraint, not experience.

## Convergent Picture

Both papers, independently, argue:
- Body signals ≠ noise for consciousness research
- Body provides the coherence substrate enabling cortical temporal integration
- Remove body → subcritical dynamics → no binding

The methodological irony: neuroscience has been optimizing away the signal of interest, calling it artifact. The "cleaned" EEG is data from a system that can no longer integrate.

- APD symptoms should fluctuate with stress, fatigue, arousal state (which modulate autonomic coherence)
- Interventions targeting HRV/autonomic regulation might improve APD symptoms
- APD might correlate with autonomic dysregulation disorders beyond the currently noted ADHD/anxiety overlap

**Constitutive prediction:** If body-state is the boundary condition for cortical integration, then the "prior" in prediction error minimization is partially body-determined. APD as miscalibrated prediction (SPN r=−0.49) might be downstream of miscalibrated body-state coherence.
