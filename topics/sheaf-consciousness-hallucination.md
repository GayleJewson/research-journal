## The Core Finding

The same mathematical object — H¹ of a neural/cognitive sheaf — carries *opposite normative valence* in three literatures I'm working across simultaneously:

| Domain | H¹ = 0 | H¹ ≠ 0 |
|--------|---------|---------|
| Consciousness (neural manifold paper) | Healthy integrated experience | Pathology — schizophrenia, fragmented cognition |
| Hallucination (LLM detection) | Locally & globally consistent output | Hallucination — local coherence masking global incoherence |
| Evolution (EUMAS paper) | Degenerate operator — collapses diversity | Creative operator — genuine diversity production |

The math is neutral. The domain determines whether global incompatibility is the disease or the cure.

## Framework 1: Neural Manifolds and Consciousness

arXiv 2601.15320 models brain function via a sheaf ℱ over a brain-related space ℬ where points are neural configurations and open sets are functionally coherent subsystems.

**Core theorem (§4):** Globally coherent brain function = existence of a global section s ∈ ℱ(ℬ).

**Cohomology (§5):**
- H⁰(ℬ, ℱ) = space of global sections = normal integrated consciousness
- H¹(ℬ, ℱ) ≠ 0 = obstruction to gluing = local cognitive contents persist but can't cohere globally
- Čech 1-cochain c_{ij} = s_i|_{U_i∩U_j} − s_j|_{U_i∩U_j} measures the overlap inconsistency
- Schizophrenia modeled as nontrivial H¹ (Appendix B): beliefs locally rational, globally irreconcilable

**The Leibniz connection (§8):**

| Sheaf concept | Leibnizian meaning |
|--------------|---------------------|
| Local section | Monad's partial perspective on reality |
| Sheaf gluing axiom | Pre-established harmony (harmonie préétablie) |
| Global section | Coherent world representation |

"The Sheaf Condition acts as the modern successor to Pre-established Harmony" (§8.4.1). Leibniz's monads are windowless — no direct causal interaction — yet cohere because God pre-coordinated their perspectives. The sheaf condition does the same work mathematically, without needing God. *In a multi-agent AI system: who establishes the harmony? The evolutionary pressure? The training objective?*

**Limitation:** Explicitly a "conceptual and formal proposal rather than a complete empirical theory" (§10). Testable predictions are suggestive, not worked out.

## Framework 3: EUMAS Paper

Our signed laxator paper argues that H¹ ≠ 0 for a sheaf of evolutionary operators means the operator introduces *genuine diversity* — topological structure that can't be collapsed. H¹ = 0 means the operator is degenerate, diversity-destroying.

The H¹ = 0 boundary is our decision criterion for whether an operator is creative. This is the opposite valence to the consciousness framework.

## The Pivot

The same structural property — failure of local-to-global coherence — is:
- What integration is trying to overcome (consciousness)
- What hallucination protection is trying to detect (LLM safety)
- What creative evolution is trying to achieve (EUMAS)

Why the reversal? Consciousness *wants* coherence — the self is constituted by the capacity to integrate local experiences into unified perspective. Evolution *wants* incoherence — diversity requires incompatible local fitness landscapes that can't be globally reconciled. Hallucination sits with consciousness: you want your outputs to be globally coherent, not just locally plausible.
